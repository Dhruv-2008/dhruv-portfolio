WHO I AM

A self-starter who learns by building.

I built my first working drone at 12 — home scrap, parts from Delhi's largest wholesale electronics market, and a stack of online videos. It didn't fly straight, but it kept me going. Since then I've built hexacopters, line-following robots, robowar bots, and a Tesla coil, while representing my school at national robotics and aerospace competitions. I'm strongest in Physics and hands-on engineering, and I'm aiming for university study in aerospace, electrical, or mechanical engineering.
